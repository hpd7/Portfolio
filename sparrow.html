Sparrow

 

Long project by Autodesk and Lunatic Koncepts - Year 3

BRIEF

Creating a personal mobility service far a public transport user to make the
commute from the transport hub to house or office bearable in order to
help commuters better utilize other forms of transport rather than making
big investments in personal vehicles such as cars. 

MY ROLE

Ritvik Kulshresta & Myself being the team leaders, we decided the timeline
of the project, our checkpoints & approch to solve the design brief.
We assigned work to the team members and also worked with the team in
every design stage.
I was mainly responsible for the 3D digital model and the prototype in the
final stages.
