Formula 357 

 

Long animation project - 4 people team - Year 4 & 5

BRIEF

Design a new experience around Remote Control Car racing
using new technologies like mixed reality and 5G.

IDEA

Gamification of the traditonal RC car race using virtual elements
and new game playes like capture the flag, football, domination.

MY ROLE

Designing the experience and storyboard the animation along with team.
Model the parts of an existing RC car and redesign it on Fusion 360.
Modeling of various assets, texturing and some animation on Blender.
Also used Substaince painter for some textures.
Sound design of vehicle noices, crashes... in Reaper.
