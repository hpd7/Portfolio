Personal Projects

IDEA

I was facinated with AR, MR & VR and the possibalities of the technologies. The role of designers in designing for XRs.
The college introduced us to designing for VR using Unreal Engine and using VRED. I was curious with AR & MR and
decided to do a series of projects relating to designing for the same, few are below.

AR Contact/Visting Card
with Animation

Got myself 3D scanned with help from Machines3d company. Animated using mixamo.com and blender. Since its webAR it can be viewed on any device with internet without downloading any app. I hosted it on github.

AR Architecture Visualization
with Basic Animation

I used blender for modeling, texturing and animating. Exported to unity as .fbx file. I used Voforia Image tracking because it supported many devices than ARcore or ARkit.

AR Product Visualizatione

Alias model from internship project.

PROCESS AND LEARNINGS

For the projects I had to understand Java scripts, edit HTML, learn unity.
I used blender for modeling, texturing, animating and keep the poly count low.
